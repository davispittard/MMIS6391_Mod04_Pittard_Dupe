About Paws in Hand Pet Store

Paws in Hand Pet Store was founded in 2024 with the mission to connect people with pets they can love and cherish. The owner, Davis Pittard, is a lifetime pet lover who noticed a lack of resources in the community for finding pets. Our store offers both rescued pets and those bought from breeders.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that all our pets are healthy, happy, and ready to become a part of your family. We also offer a range of pet care products to help you take the best care of your new friend.
